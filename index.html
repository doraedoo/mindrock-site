Company Overview

Mindrock Smart Technology Limited is a Hong Kong incorporated company focused on technology development and solution implementation.

The company provides technology-driven solutions designed to enhance operational efficiency, digital infrastructure integration, and cross-border system coordination.

Mindrock operates as an independent technology service provider and does not engage in regulated financial services, investment solicitation, or asset management activities.

Corporate Structure

Primary Entity:
Mindrock Smart Technology Limited – Hong Kong SAR

Supporting Entity:
Mindrock Global Solutions LLC – United States

The Hong Kong entity serves as the principal operating vehicle. The U.S. entity provides structural support and international coordination functions.

Core Activities

  • Digital infrastructure integration
  • System architecture support
  • Process automation and optimization
  • Cross-border technology coordination

All activities are conducted in accordance with applicable laws and regulatory requirements of the relevant jurisdictions.
