Company Overview

Mindrock Smart Technology Limited is a Hong Kong incorporated company serving as the principal holding and coordination entity of the Mindrock group.

The company operates as a technology-oriented strategic platform focused on cross-border structuring support and operational coordination.

The entity does not engage in regulated financial services, public fundraising, or custody of client assets.

Corporate Structure
